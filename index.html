Job Roles

UI Designing

UI (User Interface) designers focus on visually enhancing and refining digital products through typography, color, imagery, and other visual elements to create an aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly interface.

UX Designer

UX designers create user-centered solutions to craft intuitive, accessible, and engaging experiences for products, services, and systems.

wireframing

Wireframing is the process of creating low-fidelity visual representations of a product or interface to outline its basic structure, layout, and functionality, serving as a foundation for further design development.

Prototyping

Prototyping is the process of creating interactive and dynamic mockups of a product or interface to test, validate, and refine its design, functionality, and user experience before final development.

Product Design

Product design is the process of creating innovative and user-centered solutions to develop and improve products, services, and experiences, balancing aesthetics, functionality, and usability to meet user needs and business goals

User Research

User research is the systematic process of gathering insights into user needs, behaviors, and motivations through various methods like interviews, surveys, and observations to inform design decisions and create user-centered experiences.
